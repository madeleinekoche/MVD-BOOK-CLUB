Title: Man’s Search for Meaning

Author: Viktor E. Frankl

Rating: ★★★★★ – Diario El Relator


ABOUT THE BOOK

Man’s Search for Meaning is a profound exploration of human resilience and the quest for purpose. In this seminal work, psychiatrist Viktor E. Frankl chronicles his harrowing experiences in Nazi concentration camps and introduces logotherapy, his psychotherapeutic method centered on finding meaning in life. The book delves into how identifying a purpose can help individuals endure unimaginable suffering and emerge with a renewed sense of purpose.
