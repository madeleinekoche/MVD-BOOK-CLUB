Man’s Search for Meaning

Read in: November 2024

Author: Viktor E. Frankl

A timeless masterpiece, Man’s Search for Meaning chronicles Viktor E. Frankl’s harrowing experiences in Nazi concentration camps and his profound reflections on the human condition. Through unimaginable suffering, Frankl discovers that finding meaning in life is the key to enduring even the most challenging circumstances.

Blending memoir with philosophy, Frankl introduces logotherapy, a revolutionary psychotherapeutic approach that emphasizes purpose and resilience. The book challenges readers to reflect on their own lives and asks: What gives your life meaning?

"A book that should be read by every living person. Its lessons are universal, timeless, and life-changing." – Goodreads Reviewer (5 stars)

Tuesdays with Morrie

Read in: October 2024

Author: Mitch Albom

This heartwarming memoir tells the story of Mitch Albom’s final conversations with his beloved college professor, Morrie Schwartz. Diagnosed with ALS, Morrie offers Mitch invaluable life lessons during their Tuesday meetings, covering topics like love, work, family, and forgiveness.

Tuesdays with Morrie is a poignant reflection on what truly matters in life. Written with warmth and honesty, it reminds readers to cherish relationships and live each day with purpose and gratitude.

"A small book with a big message. Morrie’s wisdom will touch your heart and inspire you to see life through a more meaningful lens." – New York Times Bestseller Review
